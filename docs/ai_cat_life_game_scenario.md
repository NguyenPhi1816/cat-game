# AI Cat Life -- Detailed Game Scenario Specification

Version: 1.0\
Purpose: Provide a complete narrative and gameplay scenario so AI agents
can implement the game logic and systems.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Game Overview

AI Cat Life is a mobile idle life‑simulation game where the traditional
pet‑care relationship is reversed.

Instead of the player taking care of a pet, the **cat takes care of the
player**.

The player lives a busy working life. Their intelligent cat manages the
house, cooks meals, cleans the house, and improves the player's life
while the player is away working.

Core fantasy:

> "What if your cat could manage your life?"

The game combines:

-   Idle progression
-   AI generated avatars
-   Cat personalities
-   House management
-   Social interaction with other players' cats

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Narrative Background

The player is a young professional living alone in a small apartment.

One day they adopt a mysterious cat.

The cat is unusually intelligent and begins helping manage the player's
life.

Over time the cat becomes more capable and starts:

-   cooking meals
-   cleaning the house
-   buying groceries
-   preparing the player's work schedule
-   managing the home

As the cat grows stronger and smarter, the player's life becomes easier
and more productive.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Player Role

The player represents a working human character.

Primary responsibilities:

-   Go to work (generate income)
-   Manage cats
-   Upgrade the house
-   Buy ingredients and items
-   Expand the household

The player does **not directly perform housework**.

Instead the cats do those tasks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Cat Role

Cats are the central gameplay system.

Each cat can:

-   cook meals
-   clean rooms
-   perform house tasks
-   work freelance jobs
-   visit other cats

Cats grow stronger over time and unlock new abilities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Core Gameplay Loop

Main loop:

1.  Player leaves the game (offline).
2.  Player character goes to work.
3.  Player earns salary based on time.
4.  Cats perform house tasks automatically.
5.  Player returns to the game.
6.  Cats deliver a daily report.
7.  Player collects rewards.
8.  Player upgrades cats and house.
9.  Repeat.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Offline Progression Rules

Offline time generates:

-   salary
-   house tasks completed by cats

Rules:

Maximum offline reward time = 8 hours

Offline reward should only represent approximately 30--40% of potential
progress.

Active play must provide faster progression.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Daily Cat Report

When the player opens the game, they see a summary.

Example:

Daily Cat Report

Your cat has: - Cooked 2 meals - Cleaned the living room - Bought
groceries - Watered plants

Rewards may include:

-   coins
-   ingredients
-   happiness points

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Cat Creation System

Players create cats by uploading a photo.

Process:

1.  Player uploads cat image
2.  AI detects the cat
3.  AI generates stylized avatar
4.  Avatar is converted into game asset

The generated cat avatar is stored and used in the game.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Cat Attributes

Each cat has the following attributes:

Level\
Energy\
Happiness\
CareLevel\
Personality

CareLevel determines how well the cat can take care of the player.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. CareLevel System

CareLevel unlocks abilities.

Level 1 Cook simple meals

Level 3 Clean rooms

Level 5 Buy groceries

Level 7 Prepare work supplies

Level 10 Manage house automatically

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Cat Personalities

Each cat has a personality that modifies behavior.

Chef Cooks faster

Lazy Works slower but uses less energy

Clean Freak Cleans faster

Playful Increases happiness of other cats

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 12. House System

Each player owns one house.

The house contains rooms.

Rooms include:

Kitchen\
Living Room\
Bedroom\
Bathroom

Each room has levels that improve cat efficiency.

Example:

Kitchen Level 3: Cooking speed +20%

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 13. Cooking System

Cats cook food using ingredients purchased by the player.

Example ingredients:

Fish\
Milk\
Vegetables\
Meat

Recipes combine ingredients.

Example:

Fish + Milk → Fish Soup\
Meat + Vegetables → Steak

Food provides:

Energy recovery\
Cat experience\
Happiness

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 14. Cat Jobs

Cats may perform freelance jobs.

Examples:

Restaurant Chef\
Cleaning Service\
Cafe Assistant

Jobs generate:

Coins\
Special ingredients

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 15. Social System

Players can interact with other players.

Features:

Friend list\
House visiting\
Cat play sessions

Example interaction:

Your cat visits a friend's house.

Cats play together and gain happiness.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 16. Player Progression

Players progress by:

-   increasing cat levels
-   upgrading the house
-   unlocking new recipes
-   collecting furniture
-   expanding cat capacity

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 17. Monetization Systems

Free players can own up to 3 cats.

Additional cat slots may be purchased.

Other monetization:

Cat skins\
Premium furniture\
Optional ads for rewards

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 18. AI Systems

Three AI systems power the game.

Cat Avatar Generator\
Player Avatar Generator\
Cat Personality Chat

The avatar generators convert uploaded images into game-style
characters.

The personality system allows cats to speak with the player.

Example dialogue:

Player: What did you do today?

Cat: I cooked fish soup and cleaned the house.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 19. Game Progression Stages

Early Game Player has one cat and a small apartment.

Mid Game Player owns multiple cats and upgraded rooms.

Late Game Cats fully manage the house and perform complex jobs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 20. Design Goals

The game should achieve the following emotional goals:

Player feels supported by their cat.\
Cats feel intelligent and alive.\
Players return regularly to see what their cat has done.
